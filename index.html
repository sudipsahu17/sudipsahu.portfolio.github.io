I joined Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) in September 2018 and, upon successfully completing the training program, was given the opportunity to collaborate with Texas Instruments Incorporated. My first project, titled "Chip Optimization," focuses on enhancing productivity and efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing. The primary objective is to maximize the number of chips produced from a single wafer by optimizing the layout based on the shape and size of the chips.


Subsequently, I joined the Factory Automation Managed Services (FAMS) project, where our responsibility is to develop automated applications that streamline the semiconductor manufacturing process. This involves connecting the FAB equipment directly to the data center to retrieve the necessary data related to the processing.


Later, I worked as a Python Backend Developer on the GPU Virtualization project for AMD Inc. This project focuses on managing high-performance computing clusters with GPU resources to process complex batch jobs related to AI/ML, Big Data, and more. It is a web application that allows end users to reserve AMD resources (such as CPUs, GPUs, memory, etc.) based on their needs and run their jobs or programs accordingly.


I joined Persistent Systems in December 2022 as a Senior Python Developer to further advance my career. At Persistent, I became a part of the ACARE team, where I am responsible for developing new features and enhancing existing ones for the Ashion Curation Portal (now a part of Exact Sciences Corporation). The portal generates patient reports and provides clinical functionalities to support the curation team. Exact Sciences focuses on early cancer detection, treatment guidance, monitoring, and empowering individuals to make informed decisions throughout the cancer continuum.
